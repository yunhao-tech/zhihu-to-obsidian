## FAQ

### Is this a general web clipper?

No. It is intentionally focused on Zhihu, because Zhihu-specific formulas, images, cards, columns, and metadata need custom handling.

### Does it bypass paywalls or permissions?

No. It runs in your own logged-in browser and exports pages or columns you can already access.

### Why not just use Obsidian Web Clipper?

Use Obsidian Web Clipper for general websites. Use this when you need Zhihu-specific batch export, formula handling, column structure, and image workflows.

### Why charge for it?

Zhihu changes page structures and APIs over time. Paid users fund compatibility maintenance, packaging, support, and new Chinese platform exporters.
